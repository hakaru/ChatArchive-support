LINE Backup Limitations in 2026

Published: March 8, 2026 ・ 7 min read

LINE's backup features have improved over the years, but as of 2026, several significant limitations remain. Before a phone switch or account issue costs you your chat history, it's worth understanding exactly what LINE's backup can and cannot do.

1. Standard Backup Limitations

LINE's standard backup saves chat history to iCloud Drive (iOS) or Google Drive (Android). It's free to use, but comes with notable restrictions.

Text Only

Standard Backup saves text messages only. Photos, videos, stickers, and file attachments are not included. Memorable photos shared in conversations and LINE gift records may be lost.

Cloud Storage Dependent

If your iCloud or Google Drive runs out of space, backup fails. If other apps and photos already consume your storage, you may need to upgrade to a paid plan just to back up LINE.

Manual Execution Required

Standard Backup does not run automatically. You must trigger it manually. If you forget to back up before switching phones, every message since your last backup is gone.

2. Premium Backup Limitations (LYP Premium Members Only)

Premium Backup is an enhanced backup feature available exclusively to LYP Premium (formerly LINE Premium) subscribers. While it addresses many Standard Backup shortcomings, limitations remain.

Paid Service Required

Premium Backup is only available to LYP Premium members (monthly subscription required). Cancel your subscription, and you lose access to Premium Backup.

100GB Storage Cap

Even Premium Backup has a 100GB storage limit. For users who frequently share photos and videos, hitting this limit is not uncommon. When exceeded, older data may be automatically removed or backup stops entirely.

Excluded Content

Even with Premium Backup, messages from official accounts and certain system messages may not be backed up. Business-critical conversations with LINE official accounts might not be preserved.

3. Export Feature Constraints

LINE offers an export function to save individual chat rooms as text files, but this feature has significant limitations.

  • One chat at a time: No bulk export — each chat room must be exported individually
  • Text format only: Images, stickers, and files are not included
  • Limited formatting: Plain text only, with no message metadata (read receipts, etc.)
  • No re-import: Exported data cannot be imported back into LINE

For users with over 100 chat rooms, manually exporting each one is simply not practical.

4. The OS Migration Problem

Switching between iPhone and Android is the highest-risk moment for your LINE chat history.

Warning: Without Premium Backup, cross-OS migration restores only the last 14 days of text messages. All earlier chat history is permanently lost.

Premium Backup enables full history migration across platforms, but requires the paid subscription. Many users only discover this limitation at the moment of switching, when it's already too late.
